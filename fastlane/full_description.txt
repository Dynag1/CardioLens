Cardio is a modern Android application that connects to Fitbit to retrieve and visualize your health data in interactive charts.

Features:
*   OAuth 2.0 Authentication with Fitbit (PKCE)
*   Heart Rate: Visualization of resting heart rate and frequency zones
*   Sleep: Detailed analysis of sleep phases (deep, light, REM, awake)
*   Steps: Daily tracking and weekly statistics
*   Activities: Summary of exercises and calories burned
*   Modern Interface: Dark design with Material 3 and Jetpack Compose
*   Secure Storage: OAuth tokens encrypted with EncryptedSharedPreferences

Note: This application requires a Fitbit account. To access intraday (minute-by-minute) data, you must use your own Fitbit Developer credentials (BYOK mode).
